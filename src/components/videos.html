Consent Process

All participants were required to provide informed consent before participating in the usability testing sessions. The consent process included:

  • Detailed explanation of the testing process
  • Information about video recording and data collection
  • Confidentiality agreements
  • Participant rights and withdrawal options
  • Signed consent forms

Consent Form Highlights

  • Participants were informed about the purpose of the study
  • Clear explanation of data usage and storage
  • Option to withdraw at any time
  • Confidentiality of personal information
